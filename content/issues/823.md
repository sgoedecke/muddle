Postmortem for Tuesday. When the downstream returned elevated 503s, every client retried immediately with no backoff, turning a 20% error rate into a full outage in ~90 seconds. Classic retry storm.

Action items: backoff with jitter, retry budgets, circuit breakers. The unbounded queue in #861 made it far worse because the backlog never drained, and recovery was blocked by the cold-cache stampede in #877. The degraded path also skipped a guard and tripped the null-bypass in #884.
