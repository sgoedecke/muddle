The `/v0` API has been "deprecated" in spirit for over a year but we've never actually run a deprecation. It still gets ~4% of traffic, mostly from a handful of large integrations. Carrying it costs us on every serialization change (see the field-order contract in #903) and complicates the parser work in #819.

Doing this properly means: announce a date, instrument who's still calling it, provide a migration guide, and only then remove it. We keep skipping the "instrument and measure" step and then flinching at removal.
