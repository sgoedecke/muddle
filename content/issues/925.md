`/v0` has been "deprecated" in spirit for over a year but we've never actually run a deprecation. It still gets ~4% of traffic. Carrying it costs us on every serialization change (see the field-order contract in #903) and complicates the parser work in #819.

Doing it properly: announce a date, instrument who's still calling it, provide a migration guide, then remove. We keep skipping "instrument and measure" and then flinching. The frozen-serializer idea in #903 is a prerequisite, and the flag cleanup overlaps the CLI convention work in #911.
