Two problems in the field parser: (1) escaped quotes inside quoted fields (`""` → `"`) are dropped, and (2) it recurses per field, so it blows the stack at ~65k records. Big exports hit both.

The fix is an iterative rewrite, but note that changing field-splitting risks the wire-format contract we promised in #903 — coordinate there. Also the exported timestamps don't round-trip because of the formatting bug in #856, so this and #856 should land together.
