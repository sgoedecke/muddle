The same commit produces different artifacts on different machines. Unpinned transitive dependencies, timestamps baked into the output, and an unset locale that changes sort order all contribute. "Works on my machine" is literally true and literally the problem.

This is why a fresh checkout can't reliably go green — it's a big part of the flaky test in #840 and the onboarding pain in #918. Pinning needs to be wired into the SCA rule from #897, and the migration-rollback CI gate in #831 can't be trusted until builds are deterministic.
