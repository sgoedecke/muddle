Our cloud bill's cross-region egress line item doubled month-over-month, right after we added the read replica in another region. The replica is chatty — it pulls far more than it needs — and egress is the most expensive traffic class we have. This is a design-time decision showing up as a P&L surprise.

This is the same "cost is a technical property, not just a finance concern" point that keeps coming up. The cold-cache stampede in #877 also drives egress spikes on every deploy, and the unbounded queue in #861 buffers data that later gets shipped cross-region in bulk.
