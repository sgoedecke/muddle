Our cloud bill's cross-region egress line item doubled month-over-month after the CDN/replica change. Egress is our most expensive traffic class, and this is a design-time decision showing up as a P&L surprise.

The cold-cache stampede in #877 drives egress spikes on every deploy (every instance re-fetches cross-region), and the larger frontend bundle in #847 ships more bytes to every user. The unbounded queue in #861 also buffers data that later gets shipped cross-region in bulk.
