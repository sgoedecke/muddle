The main dashboard is effectively unusable with a screen reader. Custom dropdowns have no ARIA roles, the live-updating tiles announce nothing (or announce constantly), and the charts have no text alternative. We have users who literally cannot use the product, and that's both wrong and a legal risk.

Some of this is downstream of other bugs: the malformed dates from #856 get announced strangely, and the tables render badly because of the field-order coupling in #903 (the frontend also parses positionally). But most of it is missing semantics we simply never added.
