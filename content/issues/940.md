The dashboard is effectively unusable with a screen reader — custom dropdowns have no ARIA roles, live tiles announce nothing (or announce constantly), charts have no text alternative — and the layout collapses on narrow viewports. We have users who literally cannot use the product.

Some is downstream: the malformed dates from #856 get announced strangely, and the frontend parses some responses positionally so it's coupled to the field-order contract in #903. Rebuilding on native elements instead of custom JS widgets would also shrink the bundle regression in #847.
