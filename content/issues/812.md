We're seeing occasional lost writes under load in `BufferedWriter.flush()`. Two goroutines that share a buffer can both observe `dirty == true`, both flush, and the second flush clobbers the first with a stale length. Repro is racy but I've hit it ~1 in 400 runs with `-race` off.

This smells related to the stale-read problem in #948, and I suspect the same root cause is behind the phantom duplicates in #869. Before anyone writes a fix, please read the analysis in #948 — I don't want two half-fixes that fight each other.
