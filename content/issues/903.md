Several v1 clients depend on the *order* of fields in our JSON responses — some parse positionally, some hash the serialized body for caching. A refactor reordered fields and broke them. Per "any observable behaviour becomes a contract", field order is now unfortunately part of our contract.

This constrains the parser changes in #819, the date-format change in #856, and the `/v0` deprecation in #925 — all must preserve observable output. Even the frontend in #940 parses some responses positionally. Proposal: a versioned serializer so v1 gets a frozen order and v2 is free.
