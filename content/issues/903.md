state: open

We discovered several v1 clients depend on the *order* of fields in our JSON responses — some parse positionally, some hash the serialized body for caching. When we reordered fields in a refactor, their integrations broke. Per the "any observable behaviour becomes a contract" principle, field order is now, unfortunately, part of our contract.

This constrains a lot of other work: the parser changes in #819, the date-format change in #856, and the `/v0` deprecation in #925 all have to preserve observable output. Please check here before changing any serialized shape.
