labels: [bug, i18n]
state: open
author: maria-alvarez
opened: Oct 20, 2024
---

`formatDate()` interpolates `${year}-${month}-${day}` by hand and assumes the server's local timezone. For users in other timezones the date can be off by one, and for RTL locales the layout breaks entirely. We should delegate to a proper locale-aware formatter and stop building date strings by string concatenation.

This is user-visible and interacts with the accessibility work in #940 (screen readers announce the malformed dates oddly). It's also blocking the onboarding docs rewrite in #918, which has date examples that are currently wrong.
