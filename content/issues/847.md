The main JS bundle grew ~600KB in the last month and first contentful paint on a mid-range phone went from 1.2s to nearly 4s. A couple of heavy dependencies got pulled in transitively, and we're shipping the whole thing to every user on every route.

Fixes: code-split by route, tree-shake the dead paths, and lazy-load the charting lib. This hits the deploy story too — every deploy re-downloads the bundle cross-region, which is part of the egress cost blow-up in #932. The accessibility rebuild in #940 would also shrink this if we drop the custom widgets for native ones.
