Every deploy wipes the in-process cache; on restart every request is a miss and they all hit the database at once. ~30 seconds of elevated latency and 5xx, on every single deploy.

Mitigations: single-flight on misses, cache warming before taking traffic, staggered rollout. This made the incident in #823 much worse, and the bigger frontend bundle from #847 lengthens the cold window. Warming has to respect ordering so we don't bake in stale values, and every restart re-fetches cross-region — part of the egress cost in #932.
