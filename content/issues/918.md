Our last three new hires each spent about two full days getting a working local environment. The README says "run `make dev`" and then eleven undocumented steps happen in someone's head. We're filtering out contributors and burning goodwill before anyone writes a line of code.

Concretely, the setup is blocked by: the date examples that are wrong until #856 lands, the CSV fixtures that crash the parser per #819, and the fact that the auth layer fails closed in a confusing way related to #884. So the onboarding pain is partly downstream of real bugs.
